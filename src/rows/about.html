All About Our Meetup

What We Talk About

Front Range Front-End is a meetup for, well, front-end developers. Our talks will help you with:

  • HTML: accessibility, semantic markup
  • CSS: new techniques, tools like PostCSS & Sass
  • JS: vanilla JS, browser APIs, not frameworks
  • Design: UX, UI, pattern libraries, animation
  • Performance: image optimization, SVG, refactoring

Where We Meet

We currently livestream talks on our YouTube channel.

Before the pandemic, our meetup was hosted by the kind folks at Code. Stay tuned for updates on our future return to in-person meetups.

When We Meet

We meet the first Thursday of every month, at 7:00pm.
